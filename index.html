How this course is organized

Lessons are grouped by concept, not by the raw order the question pool happens to use — basic electricity and wave physics come before band plans and paperwork, because the rules make a lot more sense once the underlying physics is in place.

Every concept section ends by pointing at the exact exam questions it covers, so you always know why you're learning something and what it's worth on test day.

Progress is tracked entirely in your browser's local storage — there's no account, no server, and no tracking. Clearing your browser data clears your progress.

Once the page has loaded, the whole site works fully offline: no CDN dependencies, no build step, just HTML/CSS/JS you can also read directly on GitHub.

Source material

Questions, answers, and citations are parsed programmatically from the official NCVEC question pool documents — never hand-transcribed or pulled from third-party answer keys. Regulatory citations reference FCC Part 97. See the README for full sourcing details and how to verify the data yourself.
